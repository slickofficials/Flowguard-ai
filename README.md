# FlowGuard AI 🛡️

FlowGuard AI is an autonomous workflow recovery system that detects operational failures in real time and executes corrective actions before they impact business outcomes.

---

## 🚨 The Problem

Most businesses don’t lose revenue because of lack of demand — they lose it because their workflows silently break.

- Leads go stale  
- Clients wait too long  
- Follow-ups are missed  
- Ownership gaps appear  

These failures are rarely visible in real time — and by the time they are noticed, the opportunity is already lost.

---

## ⚡ The Solution

FlowGuard AI acts as a real-time recovery layer on top of business workflows.

It continuously:
- Monitors workflow events  
- Detects breakdowns (“workflow leaks”)  
- Classifies severity (Critical, High, Medium)  
- Generates recovery actions  
- Executes or queues actions automatically  
- Maintains a complete audit trail  

---

## 🧠 What Makes It Different

Unlike dashboards that only **report problems**, FlowGuard AI actively **resolves them**.

It doesn’t just detect issues — it **closes the loop**.

---

## 🎯 Core Capabilities

- Real-time workflow leak detection  
- Autonomous recovery action engine  
- Event-driven architecture  
- Priority-based execution system  
- Live operational dashboard  
- API-first design  

---

## 📊 Outcome

With FlowGuard AI:

> No workflow failure goes unnoticed or unresolved.
